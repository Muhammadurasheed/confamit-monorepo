The Scenario
You (the Buyer) find a beautiful pair of sneakers on Instagram for ₦50,000. The vendor (the Seller) tells you: "Pay ₦50,000 to Account Number 0123456789 before I deliver."

Here is the exact step-by-step closed loop of how ConfirmIT saves the day using Hedera:

Step 1: The Warning (The "Truecaller" Phase)
You go to ConfirmIT and enter the seller's account number into the Account Check. ConfirmIT scans the network and returns a Yellow Light 🟡:

"We have no history for this vendor. It could be a scam. Do not send money directly."

Normally, this is where the transaction dies. You walk away, and the seller loses a sale. But now, ConfirmIT offers a lifeline. A button appears on the screen: [Protect My Money with Hedera Escrow]

Step 2: Generating the "Vault" (The Escrow Link)
You click the button. You enter the amount (₦50,000) and what you are buying ("Nike Sneakers"). ConfirmIT instantly generates a unique Escrow Link (e.g., confirmit.com/escrow/ab12cd).

What just happened in the background? ConfirmIT just created an empty, unhackable digital vault (A Hedera Smart Contract / Scheduled Transaction) on the blockchain.

Step 3: Funding the Vault (The Buyer's Move)
You click the link and pay your ₦50,000 into it. The money does not go to the seller. The money does not go to ConfirmIT's bank account. The money is cryptographically locked inside the Hedera Vault. Nobody can touch it until the rules of the contract are met.

Step 4: Establishing Trust (The Seller's Move)
You copy the Escrow Link and WhatsApp it to the Instagram vendor. You say: "I have locked the money. Here is the proof."

The vendor clicks the link. The page shows them a beautiful green dashboard:

"A buyer has secured ₦50,000 for Nike Sneakers in a trustless Hedera Escrow Vault. To unlock your money, simply deliver the shoes and upload a photo of the waybill/courier receipt here."

Why the seller loves this: The seller knows you aren't a time-waster because the money is verified and locked. Why you love this: You know the seller can't run away with your money because they don't have it yet.

Step 5: The Delivery & The AI Oracle
The vendor ships the shoes to you. The vendor takes a photo of the GIG Logistics / Courier receipt and uploads it to the Escrow Link page.

This triggers ConfirmIT's QuickScan AI. In 5 seconds, our AI agents analyze the receipt for pixel manipulation, verify the tracking number, and confirm it is a legitimate delivery document.

Step 6: The Automated Release (Hedera Magic)
The moment the AI says "Receipt Authentic," it acts as an Oracle. It sends a cryptographic signature to the Hedera network saying, "The conditions have been met."

The Hedera Smart Contract instantly opens the vault. The ₦50,000 is automatically routed to the seller's account in 3 seconds, costing less than a fraction of a cent in fees.

(And if the seller is a scammer and never uploads a real receipt? After 5 days, the smart contract automatically refunds your ₦50,000 back to you).
